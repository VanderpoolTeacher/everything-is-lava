Level 2-1 Duration & Ending

How long should the volcano climb be, and what happens at the summit?

A

45 seconds (2700 frames) — Summit Plateau

Continues the pattern (+5s per level). Ends with the player reaching a flat summit area — the ground levels off, rocks stop spawning, and a story scroll or checkpoint appears. Clean stopping point before 2-2.

B

45 seconds (2700 frames) — Volcano Rim Vista

Same duration. Ends with a dramatic visual — the player reaches the volcano rim and sees the lava-filled crater below. A cave entrance appears leading into the volcano (teasing 2-2). Similar feel to the village ending in 1-3.

C

50 seconds (3000 frames) — Extended Climb

Longer level to match the difficulty jump of World 2. More time means more terrain variety — the incline could steepen partway through. Ends with the volcano rim vista from Option B.
