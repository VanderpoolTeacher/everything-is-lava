Rock Spawning & Movement

  • Spawn from right edge of screen, rolling left (downhill)
  • Follow terrain slope — Y position tracks getGroundY(worldX) as they roll
  • Rotation animation — rocks visually spin as they roll (angular velocity proportional to speed)
  • Spawn rate: starts at every ~90 frames (~1.5s), ramps to every ~45 frames by end
  • Size distribution: 50% small, 35% medium, 15% large
  • Rocks leave lava trail — small lava pools spawn behind large/medium rocks occasionally
  • Destroyed by nothing — rocks always roll off the left edge of screen. No way to break them.

Stun Mechanic (New)

  • Duration: ~60 frames (1 second)
  • Visual: Player flashes yellow/white, stars particle effect above head
  • Movement: Player cannot move or jump while stunned
  • Vulnerability: Player CAN still be hit by other hazards while stunned (lava, other rocks)
  • No stun-lock: Brief invincibility to the SAME rock type after being hit (30 frames)

Lava Rain (Carried Over)

  • Same mechanic as 1-x levels
  • Drop rate starts at ~40 frames (harder than 1-3's starting rate of 44)
  • Lava pools now form on the angled ground (positioned via getGroundY())
  • Lava hitting trees triggers burning mechanic (see Section 3)
