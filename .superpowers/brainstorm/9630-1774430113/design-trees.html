Tree Properties

  • Spacing: ~250px apart (tighter than lamps at 300px — more platforming opportunities)
  • Size distribution: 50% medium, 30% small, 20% tall
  • Platform = canopy top — player lands on the top of the tree crown
  • Trunk is passthrough — player can walk/fall through the trunk (not a wall)
  • Pixel-art style — matches existing game aesthetic (dark trunk, leafy canopy)
  • Rooted to terrain — tree base sits on getGroundY(worldX), so they follow the slope

Burn Triggers & Details

  • Ignition: Lava raindrop or lava pool contacts the tree
  • Burn timer: 210 frames (~3.5 seconds) from ignition to collapse
  • Warning phase: Last 60 frames — canopy flashes red/orange rapidly
  • Collapse: Tree disappears with falling ember/ash particles
  • Player on burning tree: Not harmed by standing on it — only loses the platform when it collapses
  • Fire spread: Does NOT spread between trees (keeps it predictable)
  • Visual: Flames animate upward from lava contact point, canopy darkens over time
