Molten Rock Behavior

How should rolling rocks interact with the player and environment?

A

Instant Kill (like vehicles)

Any contact with a rolling rock kills the player instantly — same as getting hit by a vehicle in 1-x levels. Simple, high stakes. Rocks replace vehicles as the main "dodge this" threat.

B

Knockback + Damage

Rocks push the player backward/downhill and take a life. The knockback creates risk of falling off platforms or into lava pools. Feels more physical — you got hit by a boulder.

C

Jumpable Obstacle

Rocks kill on side/front contact, but the player can jump on top of large rocks to ride them briefly (like a moving platform). Adds a risk/reward element — do you dodge or ride?
